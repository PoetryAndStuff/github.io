November 1, 2015

She was blind but didn't matter.
He was ugly as fuck.

He reeked like a corpse dead for fourty two days covered with garbage and farts.
But didn't matter because she had lost her sense of smell in a chemical accident when she was four.
That same accident might have costed her her eyesight as well.
Others say she was born blind.

Anywho, he was rich and that is a fact.
But that didn't matter because she never loved money, just people.
And the occasional scotch.

About his wealth many stories have been told.
But that didn't matter because she couldn't hear anything.
No fortunatelly she was not deaf.
Her sound perception was so strong that she had to walk around with headphones all the time so not to go crazy.
She only listened to Bach. Her grandmother cultivated her love for him.

He would have loved her greatly.
She would have given him her heart.
But that didn't matter because they never met, actually.
